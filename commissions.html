Commissions

Commissions welcome!

The galleries show the wide range of pyrography that Mandy has done, in addition to the commissions shown on this page. All the pyrography is for sale. If you want something new (maybe using the galleries for ideas), please contact Mandy for further details. Any image can be drawn onto any type of British wood. No two pieces will of course ever be exactly the same, as each piece of wood is unique.

Please email Mandy (at mandy@hedgerowcrafts.co.uk):

  • a copy of the photo or drawing that you would like to be reproduced
  • a rough estimate of the size that you would like

Mandy will then contact you regarding the costs involved.

Mandy is also happy to adapt any of the works shown on this website into house signs for you.
